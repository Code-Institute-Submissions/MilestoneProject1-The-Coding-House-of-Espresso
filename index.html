About Place

Our Espresso Bar is located at Bryggetorget 21, at very modern and attractive part of Oslo called Akker Brygge. Our place is frequently visited by both, local people and tourists. We offer to our guests the highest standard of customer service and an unforgettable coffee experience. We work with the highest class equipment and the best quality coffee beans. We hope that our place is just perfect to grab a cup of freshly brewed coffee as well as small snack and meet people in very friendly atmosphere.

About Team

Our Baristas with passion and commitment provide any information about the methods of brewing coffee, types of beans and roasting, as well as accessories necessary to start your coffee adventure. If you are interested in any coffee topic please feel free to come and visit us, we will be pleased to have you in our place and asnwer all questions and give away every single tip to inspire you.
